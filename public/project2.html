Project Overview

Project Title: Foodlicious – Comprehensive Online Food Ordering System

Role: Developer and Designer

Industry: Food & Beverage

Duration: 4 weeks

Introduction

Foodlicious is a fully-functional online food ordering system designed to provide a seamless experience for both customers and restaurant administrators. Built with a tech stack of React, Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB, it allows users to manage and track orders with real-time updates.

Problem Statement

Many online food ordering platforms lack efficient order management and intuitive user interfaces, posing challenges for restaurant owners and customers alike.

Solution

Foodlicious integrates customer-facing and admin-facing functionalities, streamlining the entire order process and enhancing user experience with robust backend support.

Key Features

  • User and Admin Dashboards for comprehensive management.
  • Seamless order tracking and management.
  • Integrated payment processing with Stripe for secure transactions.

Design and Development Process

The development followed a structured approach, from user research and prototyping to frontend and backend development, ensuring a high-quality end product.

User Research

User interviews and usability testing were conducted to refine the application, ensuring it met the needs of its target audience effectively.

Challenges and Learnings

Key challenges included integrating Stripe for secure payments and managing real-time updates. These were overcome by employing advanced technologies and thorough testing.

Conclusion

Foodlicious is a testament to the effective use of modern web technologies to solve real-world problems in the food and beverage industry, providing a scalable, user-friendly platform.
